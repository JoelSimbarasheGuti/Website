About us

Since opening in 1993, The Golden Spice's
has been delivering a wide range
of all your favourite Spices, Lentils,
Herbs, Rice, Sauces, Marinades, Pickles
and Convenience Foods

Mission Statement

The mission of The Golden Spice's is to
provide our customers with high quality ingredients,
custom blends and private label products by committing
to the highest standards in food safety and client
services.
We believe it is our responsibility to contribute safe,
healthy, food to the industry and our community.
Our staff consists of an experienced team of professionals
who work with care, passion and professionalism.
